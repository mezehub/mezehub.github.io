Decoration Refinement Guide

General Details

A G Rank armor can be refined as a decoration after being upgraded to Lv7. With the piece at Lv7, navigate to the Blacksmith and look for the Refine G Armor section.

As a general practice, remember this naming convention when refining decos:

BM GX 1 - Blademaster Head GN GX 1 - Gunner Head
BM GX 2 - Blademaster Chest GN GX 2 - Gunner Chest
BM GX 3 - Blademaster Arms GN GX 3 - Gunner Arms
BM GX 4 - Blademaster Waist GN GX 4 - Gunner Waist
BM GX 5 - Blademaster Legs GN GX 5 - Gunner Legs
