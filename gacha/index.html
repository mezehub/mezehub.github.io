MezeLounge Gacha

Introduction

Gacha Rolling is a process that allows you to “gamble” your luck for a chance at various items, equipment tickets, and transmog tickets.

To obtain Gacha Tickets, you’ll want to Navigate to the Road Shop.

  • (As a bonus, all players are granted at least 10 Gacha tickets for free each cycle. Distributed via the Guide Lady)

Look in the following section “Weekly Limited” and head towards the final page. When there, you should see a ticket labeled with the naming convention of the ongoing gacha at the time. In this case, we will be using Summer Tkt as an example.

Gacha Rolling

In order to roll with Gacha tickets, you’ll first have to find a Gacha Cat that accepts them.

The most well-known place to find a Gacha Cat is within the Workshop/Blacksmith. You can also find one in the Clan Hall.

Talk to the Cat, and choose the Normal Gacha option.

After selection, you’ll be presented with a pop-up listing the following:

  • The title of the current Gacha Cycle (Summer Festival), the 3 Gacha Rolling Options (1, 10, or 50 Tickets) and your current ticket count at the bottom.
