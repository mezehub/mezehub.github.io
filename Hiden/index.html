Hiden Guide

Introduction

Hiden equipment allows you to obtain the very powerful Weapon Tech skills. Tech skills grant a 20-30% damage increase (depending on weapon), Fencing, and Super High-Grade Earplugs. In addition to this, each weapon gets unique special effects from its Tech skill.

To gain the full effects of the Tech skill, you need 30 skill points. This can be obtained on any set by equipping 3 Hiden decorations, either ‚ÄúTrue‚Äù or ‚ÄúHiden‚Äù, in addition to a Hiden Skill Cuff. This skill is incredibly powerful and, assuming you use a Hiden Skull Cuff, also does not impact your total skill cap, so it should always be used in endgame sets.

Item Acquisition

There are three sets of Hiden items: Souls, Ribbons, and Merits. Souls are split into Low, Mid, High, and Top, whereas Ribbons and Merits are both only split into Low, Mid, and High. Each Hiden item is given in ‚ÄúOther Rewards‚Äù if their requirements are met. A list of eligible quests for each material is included below.

As normal quests only reward 1 material, however, there are two primary ways of obtaining the items more quickly. Firstly, there are HR5 and GR daily quests that award a large number of Hiden items, but these are limited to two per day. Secondly, doing ‚ÄúGuild Priority Requests‚Äù rewards 3-6 of the relevant Hiden material every quest. Priority Requests change daily, and can be checked at the Bounty Board by the Blacksmith entrance. There are also Unlimited Guild Priority Requests. There are three Priority UL monsters per day, which give Low, Mid and High / Top Hiden materials respectively.

Quest Requirements

All Souls, and Ribbons only drop from Hardcore quests.

  • Low Souls drop from HR1-2 quests
  • Mid Souls drop from HR3-4 quests
  • High Souls drop from HR5 quests
  • Top Souls drop from Gou quests
  • Low Ribbons drop from G‚òÖ1-2 quests
  • Mid Ribbons drop from G‚òÖ3-4 quests
  • High Ribbons drop from G‚òÖ5-7 quests

Merits only drop if the quest is not Hardcore, but they do not drop if your GR is below 500. They do still drop from the same quest ranks as Ribbons. In addition to this, Merits also drop from Exotic and Zenith quests.

  • Low drops from Z1 quests
  • Mid drops from Z2 and Z3 quests
  • High drops from Z4 and Exotic quests

Equipment / Refining

To obtain Hiden equipment, you need to follow a few steps. You first need to create a Hiden armor piece. These can be found in Armors ‚Äì> Special Armor ‚Äì> Hiden Armor. There are two base armor sets for each weapon type. Once an armor piece is upgraded to GX7, you can create Hiden decorations by refining the armor, just like any GF or GX decoration. At the Blacksmith, go to the Refine G Armor section, and select a GX7 Hiden armor piece to refine. Unlike normal decoration refining, it does not matter which equipment piece you refine for a Hiden decoration. Unlike GX decoration refining, what determines the decoration you refine is the color of the equipment piece.
